Smiley's Starship

Description

Welcome aboard the Terran ship of the future! Explore all the incredible amenities this ship has to offer! Don't worry about the lack of crew, they're being ... taken care of. Can you make your way through the ship and survive? We'll just have to wait and see. And whatever you do, don't let him catch you.... Good luck fellow human!

Play the Game

About the game

This was my first ever game that I created. There were a lot of strange design choices and mechanic choices I made simply because I didn't know how to do it any better. As an example, Smiley's behavior is a simple linear interpolation from where he is to a point. When he reaches the point, it shifts to the next position in a defined sequence and then loops. It is a very simple game mechanic but it worked well for what the game was. This was also a fairly big project to take on as my first game, and rewuired me to learn a lot of skills, a lot of which I still use now, however the process of learning and the learning curve made this project take about a year and a half of work. It's not perfect, theres no real tutorial or explination of mechanics, it's just a small experience and I'm fairly happy about how it turned out (for the most part)

Skills learned / honed

  • 3D modeling, animation, world design
  • Unity's particle system, animation system
  • Music and sound design
  • Story and world buiding
